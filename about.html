EMC Project by PIE Team of Rashtriya Sarvodya Bal Vidyalaya

Introduction

Hello! We are a dedicated group of students from Rashtriya Sarvodya Bal Vidyalaya, excited to share our journey and the wonderful initiative known as EMC (Entrepreneurship Mindset Curriculum) by the Delhi Government. This project serves as a testament to our hard work, creativity, and commitment to fostering an entrepreneurial mindset among students.

Our School and Background

Rashtriya Sarvodya Bal Vidyalaya is a school that has been serving the educational needs of students in Delhi, India, for several years. We come from diverse backgrounds, and our collective goal is to harness the potential of EMC for the benefit of both our learning experiences and the broader community

About EMC - An Initiative by the Delhi Government

EMC, short for Entrepreneurship Mindset Curriculum, is an innovative and progressive educational initiative introduced by the Delhi Government. It aims to instill an entrepreneurial mindset in students, encouraging them to think creatively, develop problem-solving skills, and foster an innovative spirit. EMC empowers students to embrace entrepreneurship and self-reliance, equipping them with the skills to excel in a dynamic world.

Our Journey

Our EMC project has been a transformative experience as we've had the opportunity to delve into various aspects of entrepreneurship, innovation, and problem-solving. From business ideas to community projects, our EMC initiative has allowed us to develop valuable skills and make meaningful contributions to our local community.

Our Vision

As students, we see EMC as a powerful platform that enables us to cultivate an entrepreneurial spirit and bring about positive changes. Through our project, we aim to inspire others and showcase the potential of this initiative in fostering self-reliance and innovation.
